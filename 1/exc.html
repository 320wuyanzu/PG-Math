  2. lim⁡x→3x2−3x2+1=0\Large \lim\limits_{x \to \sqrt{3}}{\frac{x^2-3}{x^2+1}}=0x→3​lim​x2+1x2−3​=0

  3. lim⁡x→1x2−2x+1x2−1=0\Large \lim\limits_{x \to 1}{\frac{x^2-2x+1}{x^2-1}}=0x→1lim​x2−1x2−2x+1​=0

  5. lim⁡h→0(x+h)2−x2h=2x\Large \lim\limits_{h \to 0}{\frac{(x+h)^2-x^2}{h}}=2xh→0lim​h(x+h)2−x2​=2x

  6. lim⁡x→∞(2−1x+1x2)=2\Large \lim\limits_{x \to \infty}{(2-\frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{x^2})}=2x→∞lim​(2−x1​+x21​)=2

  13. lim⁡x→∞(n+1)(n+2)(n+3)5n3\Large \lim\limits_{x \to \infty}{\frac{(n+1)(n+2)(n+3)}{5n^3}}x→∞lim​5n3(n+1)(n+2)(n+3)​
